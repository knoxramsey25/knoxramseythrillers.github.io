About the Author

Robert Cummer is an engineer, entrepreneur, adjunct professor, and now a storyteller. He brings 30+ years in industrial automation, process control, and agri-tech to the Knox Ramsey universe—grounding high-stakes fiction in real systems thinking.

Robert is the co-founder of Molly’s Grape & Citrus Company and serves as its CTO and COO, with oversight over food safety and regulatory compliance. That day-to-day exposure to production, quality, and audit realities informs the authenticity of supply-chain and plant-floor scenes throughout the series.

Influences include Tom Clancy’s technical authenticity, Michael Crichton’s science-driven plotting, and the modern operational tempo of Vince Flynn and Jack Carr—balanced with moral clarity and psychological realism.
