Dramatis Personae

The following entries are drawn from debrief files, interviews, and recovered correspondence. They exist here as context, not confession. Each record has been cleared for limited release. Details marked [REDACTED] reflect operational security or moral ambiguity—sometimes both.

Knox Ramsey — Systems Engineer

Once an automation architect for large-scale industrial control systems, Ramsey became known for building optimization models that blurred the line between agriculture and computation. After the events chronicled in Dark Recipe, his expertise—and his guilt—made him both a target and a translator between worlds. He approaches every problem like a circuit: looking for failure points, even in people.

Status: Active / Monitored — Known aliases: “The Engineer,” “Witness A.”

Maya Chen — Asset Seven

A data scientist recruited under false pretenses, Chen developed algorithmic frameworks for predictive nutrient analysis—work later adapted for less benign purposes. Brilliant and careful with language, she speaks more through patterns than sentences. To some, she’s a saboteur; to others, a loyalist trapped in recursion. Her code still circulates through unpatched systems worldwide.

Status: In Custody (Federal, Eastern District of Michigan) — Cooperation: Conditional.

SAC William “Bill” Steele — Federal Agent

Special Agent in Charge, Cyber Division. A career counter-intelligence officer whose moral compass hasn’t yet rusted. Steele sees bureaucracy as a necessary cage—one that keeps monsters inside as often as it traps the innocent. He’s pragmatic, occasionally ruthless, and more fluent in source code than most of the engineers he interviews.

Status: Active — Known affiliations: DOJ, DHS Joint Tasking Committee.

Janet Daniels — Secretary of the Treasury

An economist by training, Daniels became the political fulcrum between regulation and innovation. In the novel, she embodies the tension between markets and ethics: the belief that oversight can coexist with profit. Her steady tone often masks private exhaustion and the creeping suspicion that data governance may be the next frontier of warfare.

Status: Public Service — Influence Radius: Global Financial Systems.

Liu Wei — MSS Liaison

An intelligence officer specializing in cyber-biological interfaces. Wei’s calm demeanor hides a calculating strategist who sees systems as languages—and languages as weapons. His file is patchwork: some claim he defected, others that he was reassigned. In every version, his fingerprints remain on the architecture of FAC-17.

Status: Unknown — Last confirmed signal trace: Suqian, Jiangsu Province.
