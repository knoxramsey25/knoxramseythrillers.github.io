DARK RECIPE

When festival-goers collapse across Detroit’s riverfront, engineer Knox Ramsey spots a pattern the hospitals miss. The anomaly isn’t heat or fentanyl—it’s buried in America’s food network. As cold-chain data goes sideways and federal response lags, Knox races a clock measured in hours to trace a vector that weaponizes the supply chain itself.
