About the Author

Robert Cummer is an engineer and operator turned storyteller. Years in industrial automation and food-safety operations inform the details that make Knox Ramsey feel real. Dark Recipe blends the systems thinking of industrial control with the human stakes of a family in the blast radius.

Influences include Tom Clancy’s technical authenticity, Michael Crichton’s science-driven plotting, and the modern operational tempo of Vince Flynn and Jack Carr.
