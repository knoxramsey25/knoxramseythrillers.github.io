DARK RECIPE

When festival-goers collapse across Detroit’s riverfront, engineer Knox Ramsey spots a pattern the hospitals miss. The anomaly isn’t heat or fentanyl—it’s buried in the food network. As cold-chain data goes sideways and agencies scramble, Knox races a clock measured in hours to trace a vector that weaponizes the supply chain itself.

Overview

Technical Authenticity

Industrial control systems (PLCs, SCADA), controlled-environment agriculture, and traceability—no hand-waving. Sensor drift, latency, audit trails, and the brittle edges where systems meet human decisions.

Systems as Weapon

The “recipe” isn’t in a lab; it’s in logistics and process parameters: spoofed telemetry, manipulated setpoints, small errors amplified at scale.

Human Stakes

Knox is an engineer, not a spy. Plant-floor discipline meets a national incident—balancing family, ethics, and the risk that a fix in the wrong place makes it worse.
